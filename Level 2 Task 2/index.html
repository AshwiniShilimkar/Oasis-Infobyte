Tribute to "Mai"


Sou. Sindhutai Sapkal also known as Mother of Orphans is an Indian social worker and social activist known particularly for her work for raising orphan children.

Social worker Dr Sindhutai Sapkal, who was awarded Padma Shri last year for her phenomenal work in the service of orphans, passed away on January 4.

The “Mother of Orphans” died at a hospital in Pune after a heart attack. She was 75-years-old.
